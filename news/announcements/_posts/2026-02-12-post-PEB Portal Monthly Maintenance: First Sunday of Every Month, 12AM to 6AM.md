<p>Dear Users,</p>
<p>Please be advised that our portal will undergo scheduled maintenance on
the first Sunday of every month from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM (Singapore Time).</p>
<p>During this maintenance window, the portal will be temporarily unavailable.
We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause and recommend planning
your activities accordingly.</p>
<p>The maintenance is essential to ensure optimal system performance, security
updates, and service reliability. We will make every effort to complete
the maintenance within the scheduled timeframe.</p>
<p>If you have any urgent matters during the maintenance period, please contact
us at <a href="mailto:registrar@peb.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">registrar@peb.gov.sg</a>
</p>
<p>Thank you for your understanding and patience.</p>
