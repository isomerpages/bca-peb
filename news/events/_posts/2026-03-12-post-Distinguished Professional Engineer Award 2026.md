<p>The Board would like to invite you to nominate deserving fellow professional
engineers as candidates for the Distinguished Professional Engineer Award
2026.</p>
<p>The Distinguished Professional Engineer Award is given to a professional
engineer who is recognized widely as a distinguished member of the profession
who has attained high standards in professional competence and expertise
and inspires other members of the profession. The award is held once every
two years.</p>
<p>Our recipients for the Distinguished Professional Engineer Award 2024
are Er. Chua Chong Kheng and Er. Tan Ngo Chiaw.&nbsp; Er. Chua was instrumental
in LTA’s successful delivery of the rail network consisting of the Circle
Line (CCL), Downtown Line (DTL) and Thomson East-Coast Line (TEL).&nbsp;
He also played a pivotal role in the successful launch of the world’s first
driverless and fully automated heavy metro system for the North-East Line
and had led many groundbreaking initiatives, including the world’s first
integrated stacked rail depot for 3 rail lines (EWL, DTL and TEL) and bus
depot.</p>
<p>Er. Tan, on the other hand, contributed immensely to the enhancement of
Singapore’s water security and resilience. He led and worked with the project
teams and stakeholders to adopt the best technologies to ground-breaking
projects across the entire water cycle.&nbsp; Among the projects are the
Changi Waste Water Reclamation Plant (CWRP), acclaimed as one of the largest
and most advanced water reclamation facilities in the world, the Deep Water
Sewerage System (DTSS) and the Choa Chua Kang Waterworks upgrading project
which was named the Water Project of the Year at the Global Water Awards
2020.</p>
<p>Details and forms related to this award can be downloaded from the dashboard
of your PE account (under Notifications) upon login into the PEB Portal
available at the PEB website [<a href="http://www1.peb.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www1.peb.gov.sg</a>]. Nomination forms are
to be submitted according to the Word template provided. The closing date
for submitting a nomination is 20 April 2026.</p>
<p>Following the nomination, PEB would inform you by 18 May 2026 on whether
your nomination is accepted for further consideration. If your nomination
is accepted for further consideration, a detailed nomination would be required
by 13 July 2026.</p>
