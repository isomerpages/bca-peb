**Apply for Practice of Professional Engineering Examination**

* Application to sit for the Professional Engineering Examination (PPE) 2026 has opened and will close on 23 June 2026. <br>
The examination is tentatively scheduled on 9 October 2026. <br>
For information on PPE 2026, please refer to [Information for Applicants – Practice of Professional Engineering Examination 2026](/files/FINAL_PPE_2026_Exam_Brochure_19032026.pdf).<br>
For information on the application process, please refer to the [User Guide to Apply for Practice of Professional Engineering Examination (PPE)](/files/PEB%20Portal%20User%20Guides/User_Guide_to_Apply_for_Practice_of_Professional_Engineering_Examination__PPE_.pdf)

The following form is required to be downloaded for submission as part of the application process. 
[Summary of postgraduate professional experience](https://go.gov.sg/summary-of-prof-experience)
