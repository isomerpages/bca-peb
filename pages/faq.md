<h3><strong>FAQ</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>Common Queries on PEB Portal</strong></h4>
<h4>1. What is this Portal for?</h4>
<p>This portal allows users to submit, track, and manage users’ applications
online in a single platform, improving efficiency and transparency throughout
the application process.</p>
<h4>2. Who is eligible to use the Portal?</h4>
<p>The portal is open to all users who meet the eligibility criteria stated
in the application guidelines (<a href="https://www1.peb.gov.sg/guidelines/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www1.peb.gov.sg/guidelines/</a>).&nbsp;
Please refer to the guidelines before submitting your application.</p>
<h4>3. How do I log in to the Portal?</h4>
<p><strong>PEs and Applicants:</strong>
</p>
<p>If you are Singapore citizens, Permanent Residents (PRs), and holders
of valid work pass (Employment Pass, S-Pass, Work Permit or Dependant’s
Pass) and already registered with PEB, select “Log in with Singpass”. &nbsp;You
will be redirected to the Singpass Login page. &nbsp;Enter your Singpass
credentials to login. &nbsp;After successful login, you will be redirected
to the PEB Dashboard. &nbsp;Please refer to the respective user’s guideline
for more details.</p>
<p><strong>Licensees and Course Organisers:</strong>
</p>
<p>Select “Log in with Corppass”. &nbsp;You will be redirected to the Corppass
Login page. &nbsp;Enter your Corppass credentials to login. &nbsp;After
successful login, you will be redirected to the PEB Dashboard. &nbsp;Please
refer to the respective user’s guideline for more details.</p>
<h4>4. What documents or information do I need to prepare?</h4>
<p>Users are advised to prepare all required supporting documents and information
in advance, as outlined in the application guidelines, to ensure a smooth
submission process.</p>
<h4>5. Can I save a draft and submit my application later?</h4>
<p>Yes, the portal allows you to save your application as a draft and return
to complete and submit it before the application deadline.</p>
<h4>6. Can I edit my application after submission?</h4>
<p>Once an application is submitted, edits are generally not allowed. &nbsp;Please
review your application carefully before submission. &nbsp;If amendments
are required, please log in to your account to submit a change request.
&nbsp;Click “<strong>Request Changes</strong>” tab to submit.</p>
<h4>7. How will I know if my application is successful?</h4>
<p>You will receive updates via the portal and/or email notifications once
your application status changes. &nbsp;You may also log in to the portal
to check your application status.</p>
<h4>8. Is there a deadline for application submission?</h4>
<p>Yes, all applications must be submitted by the stated deadline.&nbsp;
Late submissions will not be accepted once the applications close.</p>
<h4>9. Is my data secure?</h4>
<p>Yes, all information submitted through the portal is protected and handled
in accordance with applicable data protection and security policies.</p>
<h4>10. Where can I find user guides to help me navigate and submit applications on the portal?</h4>
<p>User guides are available at <a href="https://www1.peb.gov.sg/user-guides-for-peb-portal/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www1.peb.gov.sg/user-guides-for-peb-portal/</a> to
help you navigate the portal and complete your applications.</p>
<h4>11. What should I do if I encounter technical issues or if I have further enquiries?</h4>
<p>If you experience technical difficulties or need further assistance, please
contact <a href="mailto:registrar@peb.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">registrar@peb.gov.sg</a> with
screenshots and a brief description of the issue.</p>
<h4><strong>Common Queries on Qualifications</strong></h4>
<p>The Professional Engineers Board registers professional engineers in the
branches of civil, electrical and mechanical engineering. A person applying
for registration as a professional engineer to the Professional Engineers
Board is required to hold an approved degree or qualification listed in
the Professional Engineers&nbsp;<a href="/act-and-rules/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Professional Engineers (Approved&nbsp;Qualifications)&nbsp;Notification 2009</a>&nbsp;and
acquired not less than four years of relevant practical experience. He
is also required to sit and pass examinations prescribed by the Board.
The applicant is required to sit and pass the&nbsp;<a href="/FEE/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Fundamentals of Engineering Examination</a>&nbsp;and following
that, to sit and pass the&nbsp;<a href="/PPE/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Practice of Professional Engineering Examination</a>. In addition,
the applicant is required to attend an interview.</p>
<p>The qualifications approved by the Board for the purpose of registration
as a professional engineer are listed in the&nbsp;<a href="/act-and-rules/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Professional Engineers (Approved Qualifications) Notification 2009</a>.</p>
<p>In general, the Board does not consider qualifications obtained from distance
learning, twinning programs or programs that are not approved as meeting
the prescribed requirements.</p>
<p>If an applicant's undergraduate engineering degree is not listed in the
Notification, the Board may consider the application for registration if
the degree is supplemented by a Master in the same branch of engineering
from NTU or NUS; or a PhD degree in the same branch of engineering from
an institution approved by PEB.</p>
<h4><strong>Common Queries on Examinations</strong></h4>
<p>Applications to sit for examinations are to be submitted online with fees
made by e-payment. An applicant is also required to come to PEB in person
and bring original educational certificates, academic transcripts, identification
record, etc. for verification, together with his hardcopy application.
Application received by post will not be accepted.</p>
<p>Verification of documents for application to sit for Practice of Professional
Engineering Examination and PE registration will not be required if an
applicant has applied to sit for Fundamentals of Engineering Examination
online.</p>
<p>There is no exemption from examinations on account of years of experience,
professional registration in other jurisdictions, or memberships in learned
or professional institutions etc.</p>
<p>PEB does not provide study notes, past year examination papers or conduct
courses for the purpose of preparing for examinations.</p>
